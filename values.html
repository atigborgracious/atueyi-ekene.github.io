Our Core Values

El Bethel Fellowship exists so that people far from God will be raised to life in Christ.

1. Jesus is the center It’s about: Integrated Priorities
2. We believe big and start small. It’s about: Active Faith
3. We honor one another to glorify God. It’s about: Valuing People
4. We are contributors, not consumers. Saved People Serve People
5. We think inside the box. Growing People Change for the better. Embracing Limitation
6.We want to be known for what we are for. It’s about: Promoting Unity
7. We don’t maintain, we multiply. Found People Find People
8. Focus on the Family Good marriage is a source of lifelong joy. A man and a wife.
9. Daily Devotion God calls us to discipline in relation to Scripture reading & praying.
10. Biblical Balance Being Biblically balanced makes us stable and strong in the midst of life’s challenges.
