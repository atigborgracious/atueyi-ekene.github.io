What We Do

01

It is the need of the hour, that step be taken to remedy the deplorable situation and advance social inclusion for the members of transgenders community. TEIVAH initiated let’s do it… If not today, then when?” by providing shelter, equipping with specialised skillsets and employing them. This TEIVAH equips with self-supporting employment opportunities may it be, basic computer knowledge,basic English ,tailoring, beauty Training,driving,music, pottery,marketing skills,home care,attenders or assistants to the needy,.etc as and how the requirement arises.

02

TEIVAH FOUNDATION TRUST Charity which yearns to Equip, Educate and Empower the underprivileged in the society to live with dignity and bring a change in their lifestyle. TEIVAH aims providing free tuitions or if require education and shelter to the underprevilaged children and skilled training to underprivileged youth

03

Women's empowerment is a movement involving respect, honor and recognition toward all Woman TEIVAH further drives this empowerment enabling women what they can be, what they can do, and what they can accomplish as educated women have a greater chance of escaping poverty, leading healthier and more productive lives, and raising the standard of living for their children, families, and communities. TEIVAH is empowering women via vocational trainings and small scale business opportunities in tailoring, quilt making, craft work, embroidery, clay painting, beautician, communication skillsets, English academic training.
