It is indeed an Ark-providing shelter, food, clothing,care and empower with self respect and dignity.
TEIVAH as in Word – is containers which package ideas, feelings, sentiments and convictions. ‘WORD’ house personalities, movements and communities, delineating their aims, defining their raison d'etre coz it’s only the Word which can mould a human into God’s perfect will and plan for their lives apart from all academic and recreational skillsets, both Ark-Word combined together creates a ‘Box of Life’ for both at the receiving and giving end.
