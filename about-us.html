About Us

MARKEN GRAPHIX is a dynamic digital media technology service provider that is poised to capture a substantial market share of one of the fastest growing industry in the country, digital media and outdoor advertising. MARKEN GRAPHIX intends to offer printing solutions. Marken Graphix is located at Ho Bankoe Civic Centre opposite Access Bank, Agbesi Memorial House first floor. The site is said to be the centre of Ho, the Volta Regional capital. It is one of the busiest location in Ho apart from the central market. Ho Civic Centre is fast growing and experts think it will become the central business district of the capital in not too distant future.
